Tomato Stir Fried Eggs

Recipe by: Daniel Tran.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add a pinch of salt, and beat until thoroughly mixed.
  2. Heat oil in the pan over medium heat. Pour in the egg mixture and scramble gently.
  3. Once eggs are done, remove from pan.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and garlic to the pan. Cook until soft and saucy, about 3–4 minutes.
  5. Season tomato sauce with sugar, salt, and white pepper.
  6. Return eggs to the pan, stir fry for another 1–2 minutes until combined.
  7. Garnish with green onions and serve over rice.
