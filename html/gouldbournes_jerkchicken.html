Gouldbourne's Jerk Chicken

Ingredients

  • 6 Whole Chickens, cut up into quarters
  • 8 tablespoons of Pimento or a small handfull
  • 2 Limes 1 blended 1 whole
  • 4 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 8 tablespoon or sprigs snipped fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoons coconut cream
  • 3 tablespoons of Paprika, for Deep Flavour/li>
  • Whole fresh ginger, for antibacterial properties
  • 1 Tablespoon of sugar, for flavour
  • 4 Tablespoon of salt, for flavour

Directions

  • Mix all The spices together, in a Large Bowl or bag
  • Clean chicken with vinegar and lime
  • Remove slime layer and skin
  • Coat chicken in marinade an
  • Then with blended spices salt
  • Be sure to stab the chicken with a fork, to ensure maximum flavour seepage
  • Allow to marinate for 48 hours, then grill slowly over charcoal and 10 tablespoons of allspice powder mixed in water to coat the charcoal
